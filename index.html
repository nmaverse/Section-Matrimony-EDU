The Process

How Does
Section Matrimony Work?

Four simple steps to find your perfect section swap partner.

01

Submit a Request

Fill in your name, Student ID, university email, current section, desired section, and contact info. Takes less than a minute.

02

System Searches

Our matching system instantly scans the database for someone who wants the exact opposite swap — your desired section to yours.

03

Get Notified

When a match is found, both of you receive an email with each other's contact details. Your info stays private until a match is confirmed.

04

Complete the Swap

Reach out to your match, agree on the swap, and head to the department together to complete the official section change process.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before submitting your request.

How long does it take to find a match?+
It depends on whether someone has already submitted the opposite request. If they have, you will be matched instantly. If not, your request stays active and you will be notified automatically the moment a match appears.
Can everyone see my contact info?+
No. Your WhatsApp number or Facebook profile link is only shared with your match — and only after both requests are confirmed as a mutual match. No one can browse your contact details.
What do I do after getting a match?+
You will receive an email notification with your match's contact info. Reach out to them, confirm the swap, and then both of you visit the CSE department office together to complete the official section change process.
Is this only for CSE students?+
Yes. Section Matrimony is built specifically for East Delta University CSE Department students only. You must have a valid EDU university email to submit a request.
Is this service free?+
Completely free. Section Matrimony is a student-made initiative built to help fellow EDU students. There are no fees, no subscriptions, and no hidden charges — now or ever.
Can I cancel or update my request?+
Yes. If you change your mind or no longer need a swap, simply contact us through the info below and we will remove your request from the database immediately.
